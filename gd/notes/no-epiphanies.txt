# I struggle and am frustrated and nothing is resolved

We rewatched Adaptation the other day:
> What if a writer is attempting to create a story where nothing much happens, where people don't change, they don’t have any epiphanies. They struggle and are frustrated and nothing is resolved.

Sometimes people give you advice on what you should do if you’re giving a talk at a conference or writing a blog post or something. Stuff about what those things should contain and maybe how you should prepare and so on. The people giving advice are often channeling fictionalized Robert McKee.
